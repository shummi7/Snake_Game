Instruction

Our Snake is always hungry. Help him get his cookie and bonus milk. Only one cookie appears on the field. Direct him to the cookie. Avoid hitting on the rocks.

Bonus

Collect milk bottle and fill the milk bar on the right side of the field. Milk bottle appears for every five cookies. But it will last only 8 secs. Once you got the milk bottle, one fifth of the milk bar fills up. Try to collect five milk bottle consequently and fill the milk bar. Completed milk bar quenches his thirst and gives you 500 points. If you miss one milk bottle out of 5 then the milk bar falls to zero level.

Warning

Dont go out of the field. Be aware of rocks. There are less than 15 rocks all over the field.
